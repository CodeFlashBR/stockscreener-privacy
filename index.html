Privacy Policy

StockScreenerApp · Last updated: June 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how StockScreenerApp ("the app," "we," "us") handles information when you use it. StockScreenerApp is developed by CodeFlashBR. We have designed the app to collect as little data as possible.

Summary

  • We do not require an account, login, or any personal details to use the app.
  • We do not use advertising, analytics, or tracking software.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data for advertising.
  • The only information processed is the search text you type, which is used solely to return your results.

Information We Process

When you perform a search, the text of your search query is sent from your device to our backend service over a secure (HTTPS) connection. To generate results, that query is forwarded to the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — interprets your natural-language query.
  • Polygon.io — provides stock and company market data.

Your search query is processed only to produce and return your results. We do not link it to your identity, and we do not require or collect identifying information such as your name, email, account, or precise device identifiers in order to provide the search feature.

Note: Your search query is processed to fulfill your request and is not stored by us in a way that identifies you. Once results are returned, the query is not retained against any user profile, because no user profile exists.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No names, email addresses, or contact information (unless you choose to email us directly — see below).
  • No account credentials or passwords.
  • No location data.
  • No financial account, banking, brokerage, or payment information.
  • No advertising identifiers or third-party analytics.

Third-Party Services

The third-party services listed above process your search query in order to return results. Their handling of data is governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Anthropic: https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy
  • Polygon.io: https://polygon.io/privacy

Data Security

All communication between the app, our backend, and the third-party services described above takes place over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.

Children's Privacy

StockScreenerApp is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Not Financial Advice

StockScreenerApp is an informational and educational tool only. It does not provide financial, investment, or trading advice, recommendations, or solicitations. Nothing in the app should be treated as a suggestion to buy, sell, or hold any security. Always do your own research and consult a licensed financial professional before making investment decisions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
